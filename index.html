Hourse Racing 2014, Build a Hourse Edition*

  • High Concept: This game is a hourse racing game!
  • Player Experience Goals: I want players to experience a challenging hourse racing game.
  • Genre: This game falls under the hybrid of racing games and hourses.
  • Game play: Inputs
    Super Button Normal Race
    BUILD A HOURSE WORKSHOP Customized Hourse
    Right Arrow Accelerate
    Up Arrow Shift up a gear
    Down Arrow Shift down a gear
  • Prototype goal: This prototype attempts to evaluate a home brewed physics engine for hourses.
  • Features: You can customize your hourse in the build a hourse workshop. Currency is berrys.
  • Setting/Premise: The world is set in the fantasy land of Gileneor, where players have trained their hourses for this race. The land tiles represent difficulty and struggle, while the green flag represents hope and victory. Unfortunately if you lose, you will get the red flag which is not good.
  • Story: The game story is based on a struggle for wealth. Players will gain rewards as they win races and compete agains't other hourses. Because this game utilizes real money, the struggle is made even more realistic.
  • Target Audience: This game attempts to reach hourse racing game fans. Fans will enjoy using their real life wallets to gain advantages in a virtual game.
  • Play time: This game can take somewhere from 20 seconds to till when the players computer crashes.
  • Strategies: Players should likely just accelerate and shift gears upwards at the correct times to win.
  • Estimated Schedule: Implementing a emulated microtranscation store will take a few days to develop and implement.
  • Team: My main skills are art creation. I need people with AI skills to further improve my main screen movement. I also need people who have experience with car engines, nuclear engines, hourse engines and physics.
  • Prototype Assets: Except for the build a bear image, the victory sound, and the song Voyage 1969 & Voyage 1970, all assets were made from scratch.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: This is a fusion of Catch rare fish, get reward !!! and Hourse Racing 2014.
